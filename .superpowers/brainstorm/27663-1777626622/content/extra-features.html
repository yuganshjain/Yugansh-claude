5 features that would make this app special

Beyond reading, meditation and journalling — what else moves people

Must build — core to the experience

Nice to have
🌅
Must Build
Morning Notification with Today's Wisdom
At 7am, the notification body is today's quote — not "Time to read!" but the actual passage line. They read wisdom before they read Instagram. This is the hook that brings people back even on days they don't open the app.
"You have power over your mind, not outside events." — tap to start your session →
🎨
Must Build
Share Quote as Beautiful Card
After reading, one tap generates a stunning image card of the quote — in the app's design — to share on Instagram, WhatsApp, X. Every user becomes a marketer. Free growth.
Dark background, saffron quote text, small app logo. Looks like a premium brand post.
🌡️
Must Build
Mood-Based Suggestions
Opens with "How are you feeling?" — Anxious / Lost / Angry / Grateful / Empty. Each maps to the perfect passage. Stoics for anxiety. Psalms for grief. Gita for confusion. Right wisdom at the right moment feels magical.
Feeling anxious → "Marcus Aurelius has something for you right now."
📊
Growth Driver
Weekly Soul Report
Every Sunday: a beautiful summary card — passages read, meditation minutes, best journal line of the week. Shareable. Makes people proud of their practice and talk about it.
"Week 14 · 5 sessions · 47 min meditated · 340 XP earned"
🏅
Growth Driver
Badges & Milestones
Earned through real practice: "Read all Stoics", "7-day streak", "First meditation", "Wrote 10 reflections". Screenshot-worthy. People share badges. Creates identity — "I'm a seeker."
🔥 Unbreakable — 30 days straight · 📖 Stoic — Read all 13 passages
