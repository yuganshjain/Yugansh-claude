Which app structure feels right?

Three directions for the full spiritual wellness app

A

The Morning Ritual

One tab. Every morning, three steps flow in sequence — Read → Meditate → Reflect — like a guided ritual. Goals and progress live underneath. The app opens to "Today" and walks you through each step. Clean, focused, impossible to get lost.


Bottom nav: Today · Library · Journal · Profile

B

The Four Pillars

Four equal tabs — Read · Meditate · Journal · Goals — each a full standalone experience. Users go to what they need. More like a personal OS. Great for people who have their own routine and want specific tools.


Bottom nav: Read · Meditate · Journal · Goals

C

The Soul Dashboard

Home screen shows your soul score — a living snapshot of your streak, XP, today's reading, meditation minutes this week, and latest journal entry. Everything accessible from one place. Feels like a personal command centre for inner growth.


Bottom nav: Home · Practice · Journal · You
