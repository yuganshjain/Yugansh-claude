Morning notification

7am push — notification body is today's quote, not "Time to read!" Tap opens directly to the passage.

Share quote card

After reading, one tap generates a beautiful dark-theme image card to share on Instagram or WhatsApp.

Mood-based reading

"How are you feeling?" — Anxious / Lost / Grateful / Empty — app picks the perfect passage for right now.
