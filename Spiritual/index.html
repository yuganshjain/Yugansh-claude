Crown Chakra
Sahasrara · 480 Hz · Violet
The Crown Chakra is your gateway to universal consciousness. Located at the top of the head, it governs spirituality, enlightenment, and your sense of oneness with all existence. When open, you experience profound spiritual connection and divine guidance.
Enlightenment Oneness Divine Connection Transcendence
"I am one with the universe. I am divinely guided and protected. I trust in the infinite wisdom of existence."
Third Eye Chakra
Ajna · 426 Hz · Indigo
The Third Eye is the seat of intuition and inner wisdom. Located between the eyebrows, it governs perception, imagination, and psychic clarity. A balanced third eye brings deep insight, prophetic dreams, and unshakeable trust in your inner knowing.
Intuition Wisdom Vision Clarity
"I trust my intuition. I see clearly. My inner wisdom guides me on my highest path."
Throat Chakra
Vishuddha · 384 Hz · Blue
The Throat Chakra is the center of authentic self-expression. Located at the throat, it governs how you communicate your truth. When balanced, you speak with clarity and conviction, listen deeply, and express your most authentic self without fear of judgment.
Expression Truth Communication Clarity
"I speak my truth with clarity and compassion. My voice matters. I am heard and understood."
Heart Chakra
Anahata · 341 Hz · Green
The Heart Chakra bridges the physical and spiritual. Located at the chest, it governs love, compassion, and connection. A balanced heart chakra allows you to give and receive love freely, to forgive completely, and to experience profound empathy.
Love Compassion Forgiveness Connection
"I am love. I give love freely and receive love graciously. My heart is open and healing."
Solar Plexus Chakra
Manipura · 320 Hz · Yellow
The Solar Plexus is your center of personal power. Located in the upper abdomen, it governs confidence, willpower, and self-esteem. When balanced, you carry a radiant sense of identity, act decisively, and pursue your goals with unwavering inner fire.
Confidence Willpower Identity Power
"I am powerful. I am worthy. I stand in my power and pursue my purpose with courage."
Sacral Chakra
Svadhisthana · 288 Hz · Orange
The Sacral Chakra is the seat of creativity and emotion. Located just below the navel, it governs pleasure, sensuality, and creative flow. A balanced sacral chakra brings emotional intelligence, artistic expression, and a joyful relationship with life's pleasures.
Creativity Pleasure Emotion Flow
"I embrace my creativity. I honor my feelings and flow freely with the river of life."
Root Chakra
Muladhara · 256 Hz · Red
The Root Chakra is your foundation. Located at the base of the spine, it governs safety, security, and basic survival needs. When balanced, you feel deeply grounded, stable, and fully at home in your body and in the world — rooted like an ancient tree.
Security Grounding Survival Stability
"I am safe. I am grounded. The earth supports and nourishes me. I trust in the process of life."
